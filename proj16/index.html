My Favorite Books

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

by J.K. Rowling

An orphaned boy learns on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is brought to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry to begin his magical education. Along the way he makes new friends, encounters magical creatures, and uncovers mysteries of the wizarding world.

The Hobbit

by J.R.R. Tolkien

Bilbo Baggins is a hobbit who enjoys a comfortable life in his hobbit-hole at Bag End. However, his peaceful life changes when the wizard Gandalf and a company of dwarves arrives on his doorstep to whisk him away on an adventure.

Pride and Prejudice

by Jane Austen

The story follows the main character Elizabeth Bennet as she deals with issues of manners, upbringing, morality, education, and marriage in the society of the landed gentry of early 19th-century England.

Why I Love Reading

Reading takes me on amazing adventures and helps me learn about different worlds and perspectives. These books are some of my favorites because each one tells a unique and engaging story.
