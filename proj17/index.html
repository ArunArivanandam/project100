Classic Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Light and fluffy pancakes that are perfect for a weekend breakfast. These pancakes have a golden-brown exterior and are incredibly soft inside.

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients
Ingredient Amount Notes
All-purpose flour 1½ cups Sifted
Baking powder 3½ teaspoons Fresh, not expired
Salt ¼ teaspoon Table salt
Sugar 1 tablespoon Granulated
Wet Ingredients
Ingredient Amount Notes
Milk 1¼ cups Room temperature
Egg 1 large Room temperature
Butter 3 tablespoons Melted and cooled

Instructions

Chef's Tip: Let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking for the fluffiest results!

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix until smooth, being careful not to overmix.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour approximately ¼ cup batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  7. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.

Caesar Salad

Description

A classic Caesar salad with crisp romaine lettuce, homemade croutons, and a rich, creamy dressing.

Ingredients

Salad Base
Ingredient Amount Preparation
Romaine lettuce 2 heads Chopped into bite-sized pieces
Croutons 1 cup Homemade or store-bought
Parmesan cheese ½ cup Freshly grated
Dressing
Ingredient Amount Notes
Garlic 2 cloves Finely minced
Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon Whole grain or smooth
Worcestershire sauce 1 teaspoon Original variety
Mayonnaise ½ cup Full-fat recommended
Salt and pepper To taste Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

Prepare the Dressing:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together minced garlic and Dijon mustard.
  2. Add Worcestershire sauce and mayonnaise.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Whisk until smooth and creamy.

Assemble the Salad:

  1. Wash and chop romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, toss lettuce with dressing.
  3. Add croutons and half the Parmesan.
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Top with remaining Parmesan.
